# Epic 09: v1.0 App Store Launch

## Overview

Ship Notari to the App Store. App icons, screenshots, store listing, TestFlight beta, crash reporting, and final submission to App Store Review.

## Success Criteria

- [ ] App icons exist in all required sizes for iOS, iPadOS, and macOS.
- [ ] At least 5 screenshots per platform are uploaded to App Store Connect.
- [ ] App Store description, keywords, support URL, and privacy disclosures are complete.
- [ ] TestFlight beta is distributed to at least 5 testers and feedback is addressed.
- [ ] Crash/error reporting is wired up.
- [ ] App Store Review submission is accepted and the app is live.
