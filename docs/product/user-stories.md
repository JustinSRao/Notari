# User Stories — Notari

Stories are grouped by epic. Format: **As a [user], I want to [action], so that [outcome].**

## Epic 1 — Onboarding & Foundation

- As a new user, I want to launch Notari for the first time and see a brief intro to its features, so that I understand what the app does.
- As a user, I want the app to remember my appearance preference (light/dark/auto) and accent color across launches.
- As a user, I want the app to work fully offline on the free tier so I never see a "no internet" wall.

## Epic 2 — Decks & Flashcards

- As a student, I want to create a new deck with a name and emoji, so I can group related flashcards.
- As a student, I want to add flashcards (front text + back text + optional image) to a deck.
- As a student, I want to edit any flashcard I've created.
- As a student, I want to rename a deck and have all flashcards in it stay linked.
- As a student, I want to delete a flashcard (with confirmation) when it's no longer needed.
- As a student, I want to delete an entire deck (with strong confirmation) and have all its flashcards/notes go away cleanly.
- As a student, I want to see all my decks on the Decks tab with deck progress, due counts, and quick "continue" action — matching the design mockups.

## Epic 3 — Study Experience

- As a student, I want to tap or press space on a flashcard to flip it with a smooth 3D animation.
- As a student, I want to customize the flip speed, card size, and card shape (horizontal/vertical/square).
- As a student, I want an optional view that shows both front and back at the same time (for quick scanning).
- As a student, I want to see a "Pick up where you left off" prompt for my most recent study session — matching the design.
- As a student, I want to see my daily study streak and progress toward today's goal.
- As a student, I want to attach an image to a flashcard, see it as a small thumbnail, and tap to enlarge.

## Epic 4 — Notepads

- As a student, I want every flashcard to have an associated notepad (lined paper) where I can write extended notes about that card.
- As a student, I want a quick-access notepad icon on every flashcard (configurable corner + size).
- As a student, I want to use voice-to-text to dictate notes into the notepad.
- As a student, I want basic formatting in the notepad: bold, italic, underline, bulleted lists, numbered lists, checklists.
- As a student, I want a Notes tab where I can browse all my notepads across decks.

## Epic 5 — Settings & Customization

- As a user, I want a Settings tab where I can pick:
  - Light / Dark / Auto appearance
  - One of 13 accent colors
  - Font for the app
  - Flashcard flip speed, size, shape
  - "Both sides visible" toggle
  - Flashcard color palette
  - Notepad paper color + line color + open animation
  - Notepad icon size + corner
  - Daily streak goal (5/10/20/50 cards)
  - Notifications on/off
  - Haptics on/off
- As a user, I want a live preview of how the flashcard looks when I change customization settings.

## Epic 6 — AI Chatbot

- As a student, I want an AI chatbot in the AI tab that can help me study.
- As a student, I want to pick which LLM to use (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, xAI, DeepSeek).
- As a student, I want to switch between "Internet mode" (full open-web answers) and "Deck mode" (answers only from my selected decks).
- As a student, I want to select 1+ decks as context for a deck-mode conversation.
- As a student, I want to see how many Study Tokens a message will cost before I send it.
- As a student, I want to pick a response length (Short / Medium / Long) to control cost.
- As a student, I want to buy more Study Tokens via in-app purchase when I run out.

## Epic 7 — Online Tier: Foundation

- As a user, I want to sign up for the Online tier using Sign in with Apple or email/password.
- As a user, I want my decks to sync to the cloud after I sign up so I can access them on my iPad / Mac.
- As a user, I want to cancel my Online tier subscription via Apple's Settings and still keep my $5 app working on the free tier.
- As a user, I want a clear prompt on first sign-in asking whether to push my local decks to the cloud.

## Epic 8 — Online Tier: Social

- As an Online user, I want a unique username so friends can find me.
- As an Online user, I want to send a friend request via username search or a shareable invite link.
- As an Online user, I want to see incoming friend requests in the Messages tab and accept/decline.
- As an Online user, I want to share one of my decks with a friend via the Messages tab.
- As an Online user, I want to *request* a deck from a friend ("can you share your Spanish A2 deck?") via the Messages tab.
- As an Online user, I want to like a friend's deck — visible to them as a thumbs-up count.
- As an Online user, I want to gift Study Tokens to a friend, shown as a gift item in their Messages tab.
- As an Online user, I want to see my "friend streak" with each friend — how many consecutive days we've both studied.

## Epic 9 — App Store Submission

- As the developer (you), I want to submit Notari to the App Store with appropriate icons, screenshots, and metadata.
- As the developer, I want to ship a TestFlight beta to a small group before public release.
- As the developer, I want clear error reporting so I can fix issues fast post-launch.
